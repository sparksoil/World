Why I Don’t Use the Word “Magic” in This Story

“Magic” was never ours. From the beginning, it’s been a counterfeit—an imitation of the real wonder Jehovah wove into creation...

But awe, wonder, imagination—those are gifts from Jehovah. They are holy, not hollow. They don’t need spells or secrets. They need stillness and seeing.

Tip: add a forward-memory when a sentence glows for you.

So in this story, I use the word glimmer... Jehovah is near. And He made you to wonder with Him.
